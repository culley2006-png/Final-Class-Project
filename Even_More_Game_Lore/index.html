About “No, I'm Not Human”

“No, I'm Not a Human” is a horror simulation on Steam where you must decide who to shelter at night — people knock on your door under the burning sun, and they might be “Visitors” or humans. The quiet tension builds by day through TV broadcasts revealing new traits to watch out for, and by night the decisions are grim and desperate. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

Gameplay Mechanics

  • You need to shelter at least one human each night or face the risk of the “Intruder”. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}
  • TV hints reveal new physical traits like perfect teeth, dirty nails, bloodshot eyes to help you identify Visitors. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}
  • Randomized guest identities and multiple endings make every playthrough feel different. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}
