My Hobbies

One of my favorite hobbies is playing video games, especially cooperative ones where teamwork is key. A game I play a lot is Deep Rock Galactic. It's a cooperative first-person shooter where you play as space dwarves mining dangerous caves full of aliens, minerals, and hazards.

I enjoy it because it combines strategy, teamwork, and action. The classes all have different roles: the Gunner clears enemies, the Driller makes tunnels, the Engineer sets up defenses, and the Scout lights the caves. My favorite part is working with friends to explore, fight off swarms, and escape by the skin of our teeth.

Beyond gaming, I also enjoy tending to my toads, listening to $uicideBoy$, and spending time relaxing with creative projects. Hobbies are a way for me to recharge and enjoy life outside of school and work.
