My Gaming Opinions (Unfiltered)

  • Minecraft: The OG sandbox of chaos. I can build castles, dig straight down (like a true professional), and accidentally blow up my base with creepers. 10/10 blocky chaos.
  • Rainbow Six Siege: Honestly... hate it. Too sweaty, too toxic, and half the time I get one-tapped before I even leave spawn. I only play it if I want to suffer. 0/10 would uninstall again.
  • Deep Rock Galactic: Absolute masterpiece. Nothing beats yelling "Rock and Stone!" while mining for shiny things with my dwarven crew. Blowing up bugs never felt so right.
  • Helldivers 2: A true friendship destroyer. Nothing says love like accidentally (or totally on purpose) calling in an airstrike on my friends. Democracy delivered straight from orbit.
